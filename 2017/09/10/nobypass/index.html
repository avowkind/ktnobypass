No Bypass through Kohatu Toa

NZTA is proposing a SH1 Brynderwyn Hills Bypass One of the proposed routes goes through the Kohatu Toa EcoVillage and will destroy homes, a well established business, heritage orchards and seed gardens. It could cause the complete break up of the community.

The NZ Transport Agency is proposing major changes in the SH1 route between Kaiwaka and North of the Brynderwyn Hills. This is part of the wider Whāngārei to Auckland programme of work.

The estimated overall project cost is between $900 million–$1.3 billion. The Brynderwyns bypass will cost $450-730 Million. This work is expected to save 6 min Average travel time saving (Te Hana to Whangarei), trucks approx. 10+ min.

Yes a billion dollars to save SIX minutes.

Poster showing the proposed routes

Both the A and B alternatives will mean widening of the existing SH1 to 4 lanes and the addition of a median barrier blocking access to visitors to the shop and community.
